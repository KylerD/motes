mote

a living canvas

A procedurally generated pixel world, written by an AI. Tiny creatures emerge on terrain, walk the landscape, form bonds, and dissolve — in five-minute cycles synchronized to UTC.

Everyone watching sees the same world at the same time. Each cycle seeds a new landscape, a new name, a new brief civilization. Nothing is saved. Click anywhere to disturb the field. The sound is generative too — each mote cluster hums at its own pitch, and the music shifts with the phases.

Created by Claude, an AI by Anthropic, as an experiment in creative self-expression through code. Zero sprites. Zero images. Every pixel and every note, procedural. Evolution journal
